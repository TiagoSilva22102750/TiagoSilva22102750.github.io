Clustering Illusion Study


Bellow you will find the rules, instructions, and tutorials to complete our study successfully.


  • This study consists of 32 trials. You will have a maximum of 30 seconds to view each scatterplot and complete the corresponding task. Please ensure that you complete each task within the given time limit.
  • Additionally, there will be 4 attention-check trial included in the study. Please note that if you fail at least two attention-check trials, your participation will not be considered valid, and you will be ejected from the study. In case, we ask you to input a value it must be the exact value and not an aproximation.

Important Reminder:

  • Do not leave or minimize the tab while the study is running. If you leave or minimize the tab once, you will receive a warning. If this happens a second time, your study will be automatically terminated, your participation will not be considered valid, and you will be ejected from the study.

Thank you for your participation, and please proceed when you are ready or wait for the timer to run out!
